About

I’m a postdoc in Linguistics at Northwestern University. I’m currently working on a project with Matt Goldrick, Vijay Mittal, and Yossi Keshet testing whether speech motor problems can signal vulnerability to schizophrenia. Before coming to Northwestern, I got my Ph.D. in Linguistics from the University of Maryland in 2019, working with Naomi Feldman on computational models of speech perception and phonetic learning. Before that, I studied Cognitive Science at Yale, and worked with Gaja Jarosz on computational models of word segmentation.
